Data Protection Policy

Last updated: 22/01/2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Transcendental Pong. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and delete your data when you use our gaming platform.

2. The Data We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to allow you to play the game. The type of data depends on whether you are a Registered User or a Guest.

A. Registered Users

  • Username: To identify you in the game and display your name to other players.
  • Email Address: To manage your authentication and account security.

B. Guest Users

  • Temporary Alias: A temporary name used solely for the duration of the match.
  • Session Data: Technical data required to maintain the connection during the game.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To facilitate multiplayer gameplay (matching you with other players).
  • To maintain your game history (for registered users).
  • To ensure the security of our platform.

Legal Basis: We process this data based on the performance of a contract (the Terms of Service you agree to when starting the game) and our legitimate interest in maintaining a secure service.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We apply a strict data minimization policy. We do not keep your data longer than necessary.

A. Registered Users

  • Your account data (Username and Email) is retained as long as your account is active.
  • If you do not log in for a period of 30 consecutive days, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently and automatically deleted from our databases.
  • You may re-register after this period, but previous game statistics will be lost.

B. Guest Users

  • Immediately upon disconnection or closing of the game tab, all guest data is permanently erased. We do not store guest history in any database.

5. Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
  • Passwords are hashed and salted before storage.
  • Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS).

6. Your Rights

Under data protection laws (including the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct data that is inaccurate.
  • Erasure: You do not have to wait for the 30-day limit; you may request the immediate deletion of your account at any time.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please use the functionalities provided on the setting page, or contact us at: mkling@student.42.fr

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use strictly necessary cookies or LocalStorage solely to:

  • Keep you logged in during your session.
  • Cache user information such as avatar image address.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.
